## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: OncoGEMINI is an adaptation of GEMINI intended for the improved identification of biologically and clincally relevant tumor variants from multi-sample and longitudinal tumor sequencing data
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for variant-calling tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda oncogemini`
