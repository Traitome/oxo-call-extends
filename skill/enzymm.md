## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: Enzyme Motif Miner is a fast and scalable python tool to identify catalytic sites in protein structures. It comes with a library of known arrangements of (partial) catalytic sites called templates derived from the Mechanism and Catalytic Site Atlas. Results are fully knowledge based and traceable.
- **Core Function**: Detect catalytic enzyme residues in protein structures by matching a library of known templates.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ/BAM/VCF/GFF)
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda enzymm`

## Pitfalls
