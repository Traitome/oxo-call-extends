## Concepts

- **Tool Overview**: metaamrplus v1.4.1 - MetaAMRplus is a command-line platform for the simultaneous identification of antimicrobial resistance genes, metal and biocide resistance genes, and their genomic colocalisation in bacterial genomes and plasmids. The tool integrates AMRFinder and BacMet databases and is designed for reproducible large-scale genomic analyses..
