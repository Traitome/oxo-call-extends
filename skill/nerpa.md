## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: Nerpa is a tool for linking biosynthetic gene clusters (BGCs) to known nonribosomal peptides (NRPs). BGCs are predicted in genome sequences (FASTA or GBK) with antiSMASH (Medema et al, 2011). Known NRPs are accepted in the SMILES format and processed with rBAN (Ricart et al, 2019).
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for formatting tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda nerpa`

## Pitfalls
