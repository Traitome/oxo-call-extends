## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: Oncotator is a tool for annotating human genomic point mutations and indels with data relevant to cancer researchers.
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for variant-calling tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda oncotator`
