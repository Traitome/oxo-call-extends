## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: ERVmancer quantifies Human Endogenous Retrovirus (HERV) short read RNA sequencing expression data by aligning short reads to a curated subset of HERVs and then resolves ambiguity in alignment using a pre-computed HERV phylogenetic tree.
- **Core Function**: Quantifies HERV short read RNA sequencing expression data
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ/BAM/VCF/GFF)
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda ervmancer`

## Pitfalls
- **Version**: Options may vary between versions.
