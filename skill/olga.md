## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: OLGA (Optimized Likelihood estimate of immunoGlobulin Amino-acid sequences) is a python 2.7/3.6 software developed to compute the generation probability of amino acid and in-frame nucleotide CDR3 sequences from a generative model of V(D)J recombination.
