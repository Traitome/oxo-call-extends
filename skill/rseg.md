## Concepts

- **Tool Overview**: The RSEG software package is used to analyze ChIP-Seq data, especially for identifying genomic regions and their boundaries marked by diffusive histone modification markers, such as H3K36me3 and H3K27me3. (version 0.4.9)
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data related to epigenomics
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda rseg`
