- **Tool Overview**: Rcorrector (RNA-seq error CORRECTOR) is a kmer-based error correction method for RNA-seq data. Rcorrector can also be applied to other type of sequencing data where the read coverage is non-uniform, such as single-cell sequencing. (version 1.0.7)
