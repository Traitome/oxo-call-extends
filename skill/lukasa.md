## Concepts

- **Tool Overview**: lukasa v0.15.0 - Lukasa combines MetaEUK and spaln to rapidly identify matches between proteins and genomic contigs and accurately map the proteins to the identified regions. The output is GFF3, suitable for use in eukaryotic genome annotation..
- **Core Function**: Fast and accurate mapping of proteins against eukaryotic genomes
