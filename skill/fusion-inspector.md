## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: FusionInspector is a component of the Trinity Cancer Transcriptome Analysis Toolkit (CTAT). FusionInspector assists in fusion transcript discovery by performing a supervised analysis of fusion predictions, attempting to recover and re-score evidence for such predictions. - https://github.com/FusionInspector/FusionInspector/wiki
- **Core Function**: In silico Validation of Fusion Transcript Predictions.
- **Input/Output**: Depends on tool configuration and data formats.
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda fusion-inspector`
