## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: OSRA is a utility designed to convert graphical representations of chemical structures, as they appear in journal articles, patent documents, textbooks, trade magazines etc., into SMILES or SDF.
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for formatting tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda osra`

## Pitfalls
- **Version**: Options may vary between versions.
