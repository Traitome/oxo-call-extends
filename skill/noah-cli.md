## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: Noah is a project management tool specifically designed for bioinformatics projects. It enables reproducible analysis of large datasets, making it effortless to share and collaborate with others.
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for formatting tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda noah-cli`

## Pitfalls
- **Version**: Options may vary between versions.
