## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: ESKRIM is a reference-free tool that compares microbial richness in shotgun metagenomic samples by counting k-mers.
- **Core Function**: ESKRIM: EStimate with K-mers the RIchness in a Microbiome.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ/BAM/VCF/GFF)
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda eskrim`
