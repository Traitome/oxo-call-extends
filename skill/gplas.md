## Concepts

- **Tool Overview**: gplas (v0.6.1) - gplas is a tool to bin plasmid-predicted contigs based on sequence composition, coverage and assembly graph information. It extends the possibility of accurately binning predicted plasmid contigs into several discrete plasmid components.
- **Core Function**: Provides functionality for assembly tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats supported.
