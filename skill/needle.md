## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: Needle is a tool for approximately quantifiying many queries in large collections of nucleotide sequences.
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for expression tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda needle`
