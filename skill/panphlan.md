## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: PanPhlAn is a strain-level metagenomic profiling tool for identifying the gene composition and *in-vivo* transcriptional activity of individual strains in metagenomic samples.
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for expression tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda panphlan`
