## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: The VEP determines the effect of your variants (SNPs, insertions, deletions, CNVs or structural variants) on genes, transcripts, and protein sequence, as well as regulatory regions.
- **Core Function**: Ensembl Variant Effect Predictor
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ/BAM/VCF/GFF)
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda ensembl-vep`

## Pitfalls
- **Version**: Options may vary between versions.
