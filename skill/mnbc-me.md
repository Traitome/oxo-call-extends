## Concepts

- **Tool Overview**: mnbc-me v1.0 - MNBC-ME is tool for identification of both short and long ME-originated reads across a wide variety of read types. It also identifies potential low-level host taxa for plasmid reads, and source taxa for chromosomal and viral reads..
- **Core Function**: MNBC-ME is a tool for mobile elements detection
