- **Tool Overview**: A chemical toolbox designed to speak the many languages of chemical data
- **Core Function**: Processes bioinformatics data for utility tasks.
- **Input/Output**: Standard bioinformatics formats (FASTA/FASTQ, BAM, VCF, etc.).
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda openbabel`
