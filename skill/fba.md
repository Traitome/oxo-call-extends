## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: 'fba is a flexible and streamlined toolbox for quality control, quantification, demultiplexing of various single-cell feature barcoding assays. It can be applied to customized feature barcoding specifications, including different CRISPR constructs or targeted enriched transcripts. fba allows users to customize a wide range of parameters for the quantification and demultiplexing process. fba also has a user-friendly quality control module, which is helpful in troubleshooting feature barcoding experiments.'
