## Concepts

- **Tool Overview**: treesimulator (v0.2.27) - Treesimulator provides fast methods for simulation of rooted phylogenetic trees under Multitype Birth–Death (MTBD) models, in particular the classical BD model, the BD Exposed-Infectious (BDEI) model, and BD with superspreading (BDSS).
- **Core Function**: Simulation of rooted phylogenetic trees under a given Multitype Birth–Death (MTBD) model.
- **Input/Output**: Depends on specific tool functionality.
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda treesimulator`
