## Concepts
- **Tool Overview**: PCAunzip recreates the original trajectory from the projection data. PCZdump analyzes the compressed trajectory and gives coefficients and values computed from the stored trajectory.
- **Core Function**: PCAzip compresses a trajectory, recentering the snapshots using a standard RMS or a gaussian version.
- **Input/Output**: Various formats
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda pcasuite`

## Pitfalls
- **Version**: Options may vary between versions.
