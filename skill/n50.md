## Concepts

- **Tool Overview**: N50 v1.9.3 - command-line tool for N50 calculation and dataset simulation.
- **Core Function**: Calculates N50 and other assembly statistics from FASTA/FASTQ files.
- **Input/Output**: Takes FASTA/FASTQ sequence files; outputs assembly statistics.
- **Installation**: `conda install -c bioconda n50`

## Pitfalls

- **Version Differences**: Options may vary between versions.
- **Input Format**: Supports FASTA and FASTQ formats.

## Examples

### Display help
**Args:** `--help`
**Explanation:** Shows available options.

### Calculate N50
**Args:** `-i assembly.fasta`
**Explanation:** Calculates N50 and other statistics for the assembly.

### Multiple files
**Args:** `assembly1.fasta assembly2.fasta`
**Explanation:** Compares N50 statistics across multiple assemblies.
